Our noble mission is to produce Researchers and Educationists in the fields of Computer Science and IT, along with producing manpower for the emerging IT Sector.

The Department started in 1985 with only one teacher at the Reader Level.

In 1986, a one year PGDCSA (Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Science and Application) was started with 30 seats (At that time only a few Institutes in India had Post Graduate programs in Computer Science). 12 batches of student completed the PGDCSA course till 1999.

In January 2001, the Department started a two year M. Sc. program in Computer Science.

In 2004 the Department started M. Sc. in IT along with the existing M Sc. in CS.

The Department has state-of-the-art Computing facilities and all Machines are connected to LAN and leased line Internet.
